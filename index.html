FAQ

Questions? Answered.

Do I really build for free? +
100% yes. Build your complete resume, add photo, change templates, use AI writing, see full preview — all free. You only pay ₹99 when you click Download.
How does payment and download work exactly? +
Step 1: Tap Download → Step 2: Pay on Cashfree via UPI or Card → Step 3: After payment Cashfree shows a Transaction ID (e.g. TXN123456) → Step 4: Come back to HireMagnet → Step 5: Enter that Transaction ID → Step 6: Your PDF downloads instantly. Simple and secure!
Where do I find the Transaction ID after paying? +
After you pay on Cashfree, their success page shows a Payment ID or Transaction ID. It usually starts with letters like "TXN" or "CF". Copy it and paste it back on HireMagnet to unlock your download.
Will my resume be saved if I close the app? +
Yes! Your resume saves automatically in your browser. Even if you close the tab, refresh, or restart your phone — your resume will be exactly as you left it when you come back.
What payment methods are accepted? +
All Indian payments via Cashfree — UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM), debit/credit cards, net banking, EMI. 100% secure.
Is there a refund? +
No. All payments are final. Since you can fully preview your resume before paying, we do not offer refunds. Please review your resume carefully before paying.
